HOW IT WORKS

Steps to Buy a Book

Follow these simple steps to get your favourite Islamic books delivered to your doorstep.

01

Browse Books

Explore our curated collection of Islamic books and PDFs. Use the search bar or filter by category to find what you need.

02

Add to Cart

Click "Buy Now" on any book you like. You can add multiple books to your cart before proceeding.

03

Login & Checkout

Create a free account or log in, then fill in your name, phone number, and shipping address to place your order.

04

Send Payment

Send your payment via JazzCash or EasyPaisa, then upload a screenshot of your payment proof to confirm your order.

05

Receive Your Book

Once your payment is verified, your order is dispatched. Physical books are delivered; PDFs are available instantly in My Books.
